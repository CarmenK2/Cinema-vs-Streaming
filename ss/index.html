Cinemas or Streaming?

Back in the day if people wanted to watch the latest movie everyone else was talking about, they go to the cinema with their family or friends. In case they did not make it on the movie schedule, they had to wait for it to be available in their nearest video rental shop.

Nowadays due to technological improvement we have the option to use streaming services in the comfort of our own homes to watch movies. This option gained more popularity when Covid happened as most people chose to stay home and also restrictions were put in place on public spaces. This made us ask the question, are cinemas still relevant?
